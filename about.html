My Story

I have recently achieved my certification in UX Design Immersion from CareerFoundry’s online course, and I am now gaining experience in building websites with the Frontend Development specialization. I have a Bachelor of Science in Kinesiology; therefore my background isn’t related to design, but I have always had a passion for art due to my cultural background. In my previous career as personal trainer, I put a lot dedication into understanding my clients’ goals and their preferences, so that we could work together to achieve a greater level of self-confidence and also happiness within themselves and their performance. I worked with passion to make sure that my clients would feel constantly supported and taken care of. I find myself now in the challenging process of changing careers, because I wish to be able to provide enough time, dedication and support to my family. In fact, with my previous position I would spend very long hours training clients while being gone from before sunrising till after sunset most days of the week. But now that I have created my own family, I feel like I can no longer live that lifestyle. If you want to talk and get to know me more, feel free to contact me!
